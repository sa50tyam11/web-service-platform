Case Study 1: Cake Shop Website Type: Local Business Website Problem The cake shop had no professional online presence. Customers relied only on walk-ins and phone calls. There was no clear way to showcase products, pricing, or encourage inquiries. Solution I designed and developed a modern, mobile-friendly website focused on: Clear product sections Strong call-to-action buttons Fast loading and clean UI Simple layout that non-tech customers can navigate easily Outcome The business gained a professional online identity Customers could browse offerings before contacting Improved trust and first impression for new customers Tech Used (short & subtle) HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive UI Who this is for Local businesses, bakeries, shops, service providers
